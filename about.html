A Pan-African Leadership Enhancement Conference

The conference gathers like-minded leaders, innovators, and entrepreneurs to spark new ideas, forge new partnerships and catalyse social, financial and a mind shift change for leaders in Africa. This year, we are gathering 300+ leaders in Accra, Ghana and several others online for the 5th edition of MLC.

Trade and collaboration are crucial for Africa’s development and growth.

By promoting regional integration, attracting investment, facilitating technology transfer, promoting sustainable development, and enhancing competitiveness, Africa can unlock its potential and become a major player in the global economy

How are we doing this individually in our businesses and where are we in terms of implementing policies that will facilitate trade and collaborations with Africans?
This session will be hot and tough but it will be fulfilling in the end because, we will all know where we fit in the grand scheme of trading and collaborating amongst ourselves
